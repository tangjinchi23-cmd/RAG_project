I. Spring clothing (pure cotton, light denim, knit cotton, lightweight synthetics)

1. Pure cotton (spring shirts, T-shirts, casual pants)

Washing: Machine or hand wash, water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; wash light and dark colors separately, add a little salt on the first wash to fix the color; use a laundry bag and gentle cycle for machine wash to avoid friction and pilling.

Care: Air dry in a cool, ventilated place, avoid direct sun to prevent fading; make sure it is completely dry before storage, fold or hang on a wide-shoulder hanger; place a desiccant nearby in humid weather to prevent mold.

2. Light denim (spring jeans, denim jackets)

Washing: Water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; wash inside out to reduce fading, choose the gentle cycle for machine wash; avoid frequent washing, once every 1-2 weeks is enough.

Care: Air dry inside out, avoid direct sunlight; when storing, fold flat or hang using a wide-shoulder hanger to prevent the waistband from deforming; put moisture-proof paper in the pockets to keep the shape.

3. Knit cotton (spring knitwear, light cardigans)

Washing: Hand wash preferred, water temperature <=25C, gently press with neutral detergent; use a laundry bag for machine wash and select the knitwear program; do not scrub hard or wring.

Care: Air dry flat, avoid hanging which stretches the collar; fold when storing, use mothballs to prevent insects; treat slight pilling with a fabric shaver.

4. Lightweight synthetics (spring trench coats, sun-protective jackets)

Washing: Machine washable, water temperature 30-40C, neutral or alkaline detergent; rub gently, lightly brush stubborn stains; add fabric softener to reduce static.

Care: Can be dried in the sun, turn over in time to ensure it dries fully; fold or hang for storage, avoid heavy pressure that causes permanent creases.

II. Summer clothing (silk, cotton-linen, cool rayon, short-sleeve cotton, chiffon)

1. Silk (summer dresses, shirts)

Washing: Dry cleaning recommended; for hand wash use a silk-specific neutral detergent, water temperature <=25C, soak <=15 minutes, press gently; do not scrub, wring, or bleach.

Care: Air dry in a cool, ventilated place, avoid direct sun; hang on a smooth hanger to avoid snags, avoid heavy pressure when folded; iron with low-temperature steam kept 1-2 cm from the fabric.

2. Cotton-linen (summer T-shirts, wide-leg pants, shirts)

Washing: Machine or hand wash, water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; cotton-linen wrinkles easily, no hard scrubbing needed; wash light and dark colors separately.

Care: Can be dried in the sun, store promptly after drying; iron flat before storage, fold or hang either way; avoid humid environments to prevent mold.

3. Cool rayon (summer T-shirts, shorts, dresses)

Washing: Hand or machine wash, water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; use a laundry bag and gentle cycle for machine wash; do not soak for long (<=10 minutes).

Care: Air dry in the shade, avoid direct sun and high-heat drying; store folded and flat, avoid snagging on sharp objects; avoid friction with rough objects when worn.

4. Chiffon (summer dresses, sun-protective tops)

Washing: Hand wash preferred, water temperature <=30C, rinse gently with neutral detergent; use a laundry bag and gentle cycle for machine wash; do not wring hard.

Care: Air dry in the shade, use a thin hanger when hanging to avoid snags; place a sheet of thin paper between folds to prevent sticking; iron slight wrinkles with low-temperature steam.

III. Autumn clothing (wool, cashmere, heavy denim, corduroy, suede)

1. Wool / cashmere (autumn sweaters, light cashmere coats)

Washing: Dry cleaning preferred; for hand wash use a wool-specific detergent, water temperature <=20C, soak <=15 minutes, press gently; do not machine wash, scrub, or wring.

Care: Air dry flat, avoid hanging which stretches it; add insect repellent when storing (mothballs, lavender sachets) and wrap in a breathable cloth bag; hang on wide shoulders or fold, avoid heavy pressure.

2. Heavy denim (autumn denim jackets, heavy jeans)

Washing: Water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; wash inside out to reduce fading and wear, choose the standard cycle for machine wash; soak in salt water for 30 minutes on the first wash to fix the color.

Care: Air dry inside out, avoid direct sun; store folded and flat, or hang on a wide-shoulder hanger; make sure it is completely dry before long-term storage and add a desiccant.

3. Corduroy (autumn corduroy jackets, pants)

Washing: Hand or machine wash, water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; use a laundry bag and gentle cycle for machine wash; do not scrub hard, to avoid fuzzing the pile.

Care: Air dry in the shade, dry with the reverse side up; fold when storing, avoid heavy pressure that damages the pile; iron with low-temperature steam, place a thin cloth over it, and iron in the direction of the pile.

4. Suede (autumn suede jackets, coats)

Washing: Dry cleaning recommended; synthetic suede can be hand washed, water temperature <=30C, press gently with neutral detergent; do not machine wash, bleach, or wring hard.

Care: Air dry in the shade, avoid direct sun and high heat; hang when storing to avoid crease marks from folding; gently brush surface dust off with a soft-bristle brush.

IV. Winter clothing (down jackets, heavy wool coats, fleece-lined denim, thermal underwear)

1. Down (winter down jackets, down vests)

Washing: Dry cleaning preferred; for washable styles use a down-specific detergent, water temperature <=30C, soak <=20 minutes, press gently; use the down-specific program and a laundry bag for machine wash.

Care: Air dry in a ventilated, cool place, gently pat to restore fluffiness while drying; store after it is completely dry, do not over-compress in a compression bag; take it out periodically to pat and air it.

2. Heavy wool coats (winter wool coats, tweed overcoats)

Washing: Must be dry cleaned; dry cleaning protects the elasticity and softness of the wool fibers; do not water wash or machine wash, to avoid felting and shrinkage.

Care: Store hanging on a wide-shoulder no-crease hanger, away from humidity and heat; pat off dust before storage and add insect repellent; avoid snagging on sharp objects.

3. Fleece-lined denim (winter fleece jeans, fleece denim jackets)

Washing: Water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; wash inside out, choose the gentle cycle for machine wash; avoid long soaking to reduce shedding of the fleece layer.

Care: Air dry inside out, avoid direct sun; store folded and flat, avoid heavy pressure that damages the fleece layer; dry promptly after washing to prevent mold and odor.

4. Thermal underwear (pure cotton thermal underwear, brushed thermal underwear)

Washing: Machine or hand wash, water temperature <=30C, neutral detergent; choose the gentle cycle for machine wash, avoid strong spinning; do not use bleach.

Care: Air dry in the shade or briefly in the sun; store folded flat in a dry place; avoid high-temperature ironing on brushed fabrics to protect the thermal fibers.

V. General care tips

1. Wash garments of different materials separately to avoid dyeing and wear; fully dissolve the detergent before adding the clothes to avoid local discoloration.

2. Treat stains promptly, the longer they sit the harder they are to remove; treat different stains accordingly (undiluted dish soap for grease, cold-water soaking for blood).

3. Check the care label before ironing and adjust the temperature by material; test on the inside of the garment first to avoid scorching.

4. For long-term storage, make sure the garment is completely dry before storing, and check periodically for mold and insect damage.
